This application allows to listen to Maxi80, the premiere 80's french web radio.

Maxi80 provides a high quality sound 24/7 with the best music from the eighties

This application allows you to

- pause and resume listening
- adjust volume
- display the artist name and song title currently on air
- display the original cover when available
- send an email to the radio station
- share cover artwork with other apps such as iMessage, Facebook, Twitter (when installed)
